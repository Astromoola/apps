Delete Your Data

You’re in control of your astrology data. Below are the steps to completely remove any information you’ve entered into Navya Mandala.

1. Delete Local Profiles

  • Open the Profiles screen in-app.
  • Tap the delete icon.
  • This permanently erases that profile and all related chart data from your device.

2. Remove Login (Firebase) Credentials

  • Go to the app’s side menu (≡) and tap Delete Account.
  • You may be asked to sign in again to confirm. After success, your authentication entries (Apple/Google) are revoked.
  • No birth data is stored on our servers—only your login token is removed.
Note: We do not back up your profiles to any remote server. Once deleted locally, your data cannot be recovered.
